Perfect Pizza

Description

Why spend money on take out pizza when you can cook it at home for a fraction of the price? Homemade pizza might sound intimidating but it's really as easy as pie!

There are a few reccomended items for this recipe such as, a rolling pin, pizza stone and a pizza peel, but if you dont have those you can roll out you dough with a glass bottle on your baking sheet. Also, this recipe calls for pepperoni but you are welcome to substitute your own toppings.

Ingredients

  • store bought pizza dough
  • flour
  • pizza sauce
  • shredded cheese
  • pepperoni
  • toppings of your choice

Steps

  1. Place pizza stone in oven and heat to 450 degrees ferinheit
  2. Spread flour on peel or baking sheet and roll out dough to desired size
  3. Spread pizza sauce over dough evenly
  4. Add toppings of choice and place in oven for 10 minutes or until crispy
  5. Carefully remove pizza and slice immediatly
  6. Enjoy!
