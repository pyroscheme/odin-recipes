Seared Scallops

Description

Want to feel like a 5-star chef at home? Try cooking this wonderfully Delicious yet easy to make butter basted scallops. To start, you will want to purchase 1 pound of large scallops. Avoid supermarkets when searching for where to buy these. You will want to find a local fish market for best quality. Additionally, you will want to use unsalted butter for basting and optionally garlic and thyme for an enhanced flavor. Be sure to also have some olive oil on hand for searing. It is recommeded that you use a cast iron skillet to obtain optimal searing.

Don't worry if you don't have everything previously described. Your scallops will be fine! Scallops are delicious on their own, we just like to enhance them with the simple tips and additions we mentioned earlier.

Ingredients

  • scallops
  • butter
  • garlic
  • thyme
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Heat pan over high heat and add olive oil
  2. Once slightly smoking, add scallops and sear
  3. Flip once crust has developed and add butter to pan with garlic and thyme
  4. Baste for an additional 2 minutes and remove scallops from pan with juices
  5. Enjoy!
